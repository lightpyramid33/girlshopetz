About Us

FOUNDATION FOR GIRL'S HOPE-(FGH) is a child focused,Non-Government Organization registered 2019 under the Non-Government Organization Act 2002, with registration No ooNGO/R/096 to work in Tanzania mainland. The organization focuss on the protection and elimination of all form of abuse and stimatization to children, advocating on issues of children in the community and awareness raisingon basic children's rights,gender equality and equity.
Before registration in julu 2019,foundation for girl's hope started as a group which focused on championing child rights and protection especially on right ot education to children and young girls.
